About DivSprout

Built by an investor,
for investors.

What is DivSprout?

DivSprout is a free toolkit for dividend investors. The goal is simple: give retail investors the same quality of financial tools that used to be locked behind expensive platforms or buried under paywalls and popup ads.

Every calculator on DivSprout runs entirely in your browser. No data is sent to any server. No account is required. No email needed. You close the tab, and nothing is stored anywhere.

Why we built this

Dividend investing requires running a lot of numbers — yield, reinvestment growth, income projections, FIRE targets. Most free tools online are either broken, cluttered with advertising, or force you to create an account before showing you a result.

DivSprout exists because those tools frustrated us as investors. We wanted something fast, honest, and private. So we built it ourselves and made it free for everyone.

Our principles

Zero data collection
All calculations happen in your browser. We never see your numbers, your portfolio, or your email.
Instant results
No loading spinners, no submit buttons. Every result updates in real-time as you type.
Always free
Core tools will always be free. We're transparent about how the site is funded (see below).
No BS content
No fake urgency, no misleading headlines, no "CLICK HERE TO GET RICH" nonsense. Just tools.

How DivSprout is funded

DivSprout is currently self-funded. In the future, the site may include clearly labelled affiliate links to brokerages — meaning if you open an account through a link on this site, we may receive a referral fee at no cost to you. Any affiliate relationship will always be disclosed clearly on the relevant page.

Affiliate links never influence calculator results, tool recommendations, or editorial content. A broker will not appear on DivSprout because they paid to be there. Period.
