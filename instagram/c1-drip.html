What is DRIP?
Dividend Reinvestment Plan
Definition
Instead of receiving dividends as cash, DRIP automatically buys more shares with each payout — compounding your ownership over time.
1
You receive a dividend payout from your shares
2
Instead of cash, it auto-buys fractional shares
3
More shares → more dividends next quarter
4
The cycle repeats — compounding accelerates
