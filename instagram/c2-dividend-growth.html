Warning signs
When high yield
is a danger signal
📉
Yield over 8–10%
Often signals a falling share price, not generosity. The market is pricing in a cut.
⚡
Payout ratio above 80%
Company is paying out most of its earnings — no room to raise or sustain dividends.
🔻
Dividend hasn't grown in 5+ years
Inflation silently erodes your real income. A "safe" 6% becomes a 4% real yield over a decade.
🏚️
Declining free cash flow
Dividends are only as safe as the cash flow backing them. Always check FCF coverage.
