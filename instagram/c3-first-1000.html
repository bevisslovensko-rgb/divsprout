Before you invest
2 things to check
off first
🛡️
Emergency fund: 3–6 months of expenses
Investing money you might need soon forces panic selling. Build your safety net first — high-yield savings account.
💳
Pay off high-interest debt first
No dividend stock reliably returns 20%+. If you have credit card debt at 18–22% APR, paying it off is the best investment you can make.
✅
Ready? Start with money you won't need for 5+ years
Dividend investing rewards patience. Market dips feel very different when you don't need the cash.

Account types
Pick the right
account first
🌟
Roth IRA — Start here
Tax-free growth forever. Dividends compound inside, no annual tax drag. $7,000/yr limit.
Best for long-term compounding
🏢
401(k) / employer plan
Contribute enough to get the full employer match first — that's a guaranteed 50–100% return on your dollars.
📈
Taxable brokerage account
After maxing tax-advantaged accounts. Dividend income is taxed annually — still great, just less tax-efficient.
