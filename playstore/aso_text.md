💰 DivSprout — Your Dividend & FIRE Calculator

Turn your investments into passive income. DivSprout calculates exactly how much monthly dividend income your portfolio will generate after any number of years — factoring in compound growth, regular contributions, and dividend reinvestment.

Whether you're building towards financial independence, planning for early retirement, or simply want to know how your dividend portfolio will grow, DivSprout gives you clear, honest projections in seconds.

─────────────────────────────────────────
📊 DIVIDEND INCOME CALCULATOR
─────────────────────────────────────────
• Enter your starting capital, monthly contribution, dividend yield, and investment horizon
• See projected monthly and annual dividend income after 1, 5, 10, 20, or 30 years
• DRIP (Dividend Reinvestment Plan) compounding built in — watch your income snowball
• Instant recalculation as you adjust any input

─────────────────────────────────────────
🌱 PORTFOLIO GROWTH PROJECTIONS
─────────────────────────────────────────
• Visual growth chart showing portfolio value over time
• Compare multiple scenarios side by side — conservative, moderate, and aggressive yield assumptions
• Milestone tracker: see exactly when you hit $500/mo, $1 000/mo, $2 000/mo in dividends
• Total return breakdown: contributed capital vs. reinvested dividends vs. price appreciation

─────────────────────────────────────────
🔥 FIRE GOAL TRACKER
─────────────────────────────────────────
• Set your monthly passive income target (your "FIRE number")
• Live progress bar shows how close you are to financial independence
• Calculates the exact year you reach your goal based on current plan
• Supports both lean FIRE and fat FIRE scenarios

─────────────────────────────────────────
📤 SHARE YOUR PROGRESS
