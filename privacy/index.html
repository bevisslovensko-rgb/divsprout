Privacy Policy

Your data is yours.
We never see it.

Last updated: May 2026

Short version: DivSprout collects zero personal data. All calculations run entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server. No account, no cookies, no tracking.

What we collect

Nothing. DivSprout does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All calculator inputs — your portfolio values, dividend yields, contribution amounts — exist only in your browser's memory and are discarded when you close the tab.

Cookies

DivSprout does not set any cookies. We do not use session cookies, tracking cookies, or any persistent local storage to identify you across visits.

If you use the "Copy link" feature in a calculator, your inputs are encoded in the URL as query parameters — this data lives only in your browser's address bar and is never sent to our servers unless you explicitly share the URL with someone else.

Analytics

We may use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand aggregate traffic patterns (total page views, which pages are visited). Cloudflare's analytics is privacy-preserving: it does not use cookies, does not fingerprint individual users, and does not track visitors across sites. No personally identifiable information is collected. You can review Cloudflare's privacy policy at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

Third-party services

DivSprout loads fonts from Google Fonts. When your browser requests a font file, Google's servers receive your IP address as part of the standard HTTP request. We do not control this interaction. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

No other third-party scripts, pixels, or SDKs are loaded on this site.

Affiliate links

DivSprout may include clearly labelled affiliate links to brokerages and financial services. If you click an affiliate link, you will be directed to a third-party website. That website operates under its own privacy policy. DivSprout does not share any information about you with affiliate partners — we have no information to share.

Affiliate links are always disclosed on the page where they appear. They do not influence calculator results, tool rankings, or editorial content.

Children's privacy

DivSprout is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Because we collect no data, changes are unlikely to affect your privacy in any way.

Contact

Questions about this policy? A contact form is coming soon. DivSprout is a one-person project — all tools are free and open to use without an account.
